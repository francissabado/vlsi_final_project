EE103: Introduction to VLSI Design

Final Project: Streamlined 8-bit Adder
by Nicholas Davis and Noah Kurinsky


Abstract

We propose to develop an 8-bit CMOS adder with look ahead carry, with the design goals of maximizing performance through careful stage sizing while minimizing chip area and sources of leakage current. We will additionally add subtraction capabilities if possible through s complement subtraction, and propose to optimize performance through careful design of crucial bottlenecks and smart carry treatment.
